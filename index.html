About Walmart SmartShop

We've all experienced the stress when it comes to finding what we need in large stores. Searching for one item among hundreds or even thousands of products can be intimidating. With Walmart SmartShop, we hope to combat this challenge.

GPS Assisted

Bluetooth and GPS - based indoor mapping component that displays one's location and aisle as a pulsating, blue dot.

Product Finder

The app calculates and displays the most optimal path to user's desired product using constantly updating path-finding algorithms.

At the Touch of a Button

Items can be selected via the Walmart app, providing a connection between the 2 worlds of online and in-store shopping.

Accessibility

We aim to make the app as accessible as possible to Walmart's diverse group of millions of customers worldwide.

  • Available support for keyboard interactions and screen readers
  • User customization options including font size, display color, and screen magnification
  • Audio-assisted descriptions for item selection and audio directions for finding products in-store
  • Descriptions and directions provided in dozens of international languages

Security

It is of utmost importance to protect our user's security.

  • Data encryption
  • High level authentication
  • Tamper-detection technologies
  • Best cryptography tools and techniques

User Feedback

We hope to continuously improve our app through integrating user feedback.

  • Option to resport mis-located items and inaccurate path finding
  • Accessibility and app features feedback
  • Item location recommendations

We strive to make our customers as happy as possible :)

Frequent Updates

We hope to keep the item directory as accurate to the present situation as possible.

  • Options for store staff to edit items in directory list and their corresponding locations
  • User feedback integrated into app once approved by staff
  • Item mis-location errors fixed on a daily basis
